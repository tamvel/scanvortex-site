GS1 ScanVortex is a professional barcode scanner designed for working with GS1 standards, including GS1 DataMatrix and GS1-128.

The app allows you to quickly scan, validate and analyze GS1 barcodes. It automatically parses Application Identifiers (AI) and clearly displays structured data such as:

  • GTIN (Global Trade Item Number)
  • Batch / Lot number
  • Expiration date
  • Production date
  • Serial number
  • Quantity and other GS1 elements

Key features

  • Fast and accurate GS1 barcode scanning
  • Automatic AI (Application Identifier) parsing
  • GS1 format validation
  • Clear structured data presentation
  • Scan history for quick reference

Use cases

  • Warehouse and logistics operations
  • Production and quality control
  • Supply chain verification
